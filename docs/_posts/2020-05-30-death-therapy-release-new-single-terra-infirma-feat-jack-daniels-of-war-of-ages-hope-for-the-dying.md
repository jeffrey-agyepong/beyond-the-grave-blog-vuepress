**Death Therapy** (formed by Jason Wisdom ex.**Becoming The Archetype**) have released their second new single entitled, “Terra Infirma” from their upcoming EP; “**Dance Therapy: Pre-Apocalyptic Cyber Funk For Late Stage Humanoids**“. This single features legendary guitarist **Jack Daniels** of **Hope For the Dying** & **War of Ages** near the tail end with an amazing solo. This upcoming EP is the first of two EPs set to be released this year. This one is purely instrumental tracks. Check it out and if you like it, consider pre-ordering it _[here](https://deaththerapy.bigcartel.com)_. He's also releasing a _[devotional](https://deaththerapy.bigcartel.com/product/pre-order-devotional-book-written-by-jason-wisdom)_ book, so you might also be interested in getting that as well.
